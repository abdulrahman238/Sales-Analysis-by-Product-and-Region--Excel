# Sales Analysis by Product and Region

![Sales Dashboard](./Default%20Sheet%20Image.png)

This analysis is based on the Pivot Table showing the percentage contribution of each product to total sales across four regions: East, North, South, and West.

## Key Insights

### Top Performing Products
- **Laptop** is the strongest performer overall, contributing **29%** to total sales. It dominates in **North (41.81%)** and **West (24.90%)**.  
- **Printer** and **Office Desk** are mid-performing products, contributing **8.84%** and **11.46%** respectively.

### Low Performing Products
- **Marker Set** consistently underperforms (<0.3%), suggesting very low demand.  
- **Notebook** also contributes minimally (<0.5%), indicating either low sales or low priority.

### Regional Performance Patterns
- **North**: Laptops lead (41.81%).  
- **South**: Smartphones dominate (32.56%).  
- **East**: Laptops (26.14%) and Printers (17.24%) lead moderately.  
- **West**: Sales are balanced, with Laptops (24.90%) and Office Desks (16.32%) performing well.

### Products with Regional Disparities
- **Desk Chair**: Strong in East (10.50%) and South (10.19%) but low in North (5.02%) and West (6.80%).  
- **Filing Cabinet**: Performs better in North (6.68%) but poorly in other regions.  

This indicates opportunities for **targeted marketing and regional stock optimization**.

### Overall Observations
- High-value products like **Laptops** drive the majority of sales.  
- Low-performing products may need marketing improvement, promotion, or possible discontinuation.  
- Regional sales patterns suggest **regional-focused strategies** can improve overall performance.
